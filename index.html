About Us

Al Rakhie International Co. Ltd for drilling wells is a Sudanese-Saudi company established in 1986 in Sudan and 1970 in Saudi Arabia. The company performs in the following areas:

  • a. Drilling water wells, cleaning & maintaining wells, implementation of water supply systems by Al Rakhie.
  • b. Import of drilling materials for sales and operation by Al Rakhie Commercial Ltd.

Drilling Section

The company owns a group of water well rigs operating all over Sudan, equipped with modern technology (Gardner Denver). Out of those drilling rigs which are conventional rotary, others are Hammer Drills. Each machine has a trailer for transportation of water and pipes in addition to a small emergency car, generator, welding machines, drilling bars, and many kinds of keys.

Description of Drilling Machines

  • Rotary Drill: Some of these Rotary Drilling machines have a 3000 feet (1000 m) capacity, equipped with Double prime mover (Detroit Engine), Mud pump (7.5” x 10”) hook with capacity of 40 tons, and retractable rotary table.
  • Others have a 1500 feet capacity, equipped with Detroit Engine and Mud pump (5.5” x 8”).
  • Gardner Denver Rotary Drill: Some of these Drilling Rigs have a 2000 feet capacity, equipped with compressor 1000 CFM 750 psi, with an injection pump for foam.

Water Well Development & Maintenance

The company owns more than one water well development machine, each mounted with a drilling rig, bailer, and air compressor (350 Psi, 1000 CFM), along with a small car equipped with all necessary tools: welding machine, generator, pump, and water level measuring equipment.

The company employs experienced engineers in this field and has successfully cleaned wells in various parts of Sudan — including 20-year-old wells such as those in Muglad and Gezira state, which are now fully productive and in excellent condition.

Water Supply System Section

The company has conducted numerous studies and mapping surveys for installing water supply systems in towns and villages. With a team of specialized engineers, Al Rakhie has successfully implemented a 6-kilometer water supply system in Haya City, Red Sea State.
